About

Food Image Classifier helps you identify dishes from a photo. Just upload a picture of food and we’ll tell you what it most likely is, along with how confident we are.

It’s designed for everyone—no tech know‑how needed. Great for learning about cuisines, checking what’s on your plate, or exploring new dishes.

How to use it:

  • Choose a food photo from your device
  • Click “Classify Image”
  • See the predicted dish and confidence
